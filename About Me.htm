hello

I'm Kristen and I'm the owner of Baked to Confection, a small, in-home bakery specializing in cookies. I started making cookies for fun in March 2018 and I enjoyed it so much that it became a frequent hobby of mine. Eventually friends and family started ordering them, so I opened orders to the local public and was pleasantly surprised with how quickly the orders rolled in! In the time since I opened, my menu has expanded and my love for baking (and my amazing customers!) has grown exponentially. If you have any questions or want to place a special order, please get in touch! I'd be more than happy to help design something you'll love. Thank you for letting me be a part of your special event!
